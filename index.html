Crush Game Development and Design

The game development club at Clovis Community College (CCC) in Fresno. Learn how games are made, join game jams, and build projects together. Whether you code, draw, make music, or just love games, there's a place for you here.

About the Club

Who We Are

A community of Clovis Community College students who want to learn how games are made.

What We Do

We do many projects from big to small with awesome collaboration between artists, musicians, programmers, and designers.

Why Join

Build a portfolio, make friends who share your passion, collaborate on real projects, and pick up skills that carry into the game industry and beyond.

Meetings

We meet every Friday on campus at Clovis Community College throughout the Fall and Spring semesters. Drop in to work on projects, get feedback, or just see what we're about. Everyone is welcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to join?

Not at all. We welcome complete beginners and experienced developers alike. The whole point is to learn.

I'm not a programmer. Can I still contribute?

Absolutely. Games need artists, writers, musicians, designers, and testers just as much as programmers.

What tools and engines do you use?

We use engines like Godot, which is open source, but you are free to use other engines if the programmer lead(s) agree.

Join Us

Whether you want to ship your first game or your fifth, we'd love to have you. Reach out and we'll get you the meeting details and add you to the community. Open to all Clovis Community College students!
